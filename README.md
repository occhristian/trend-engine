# Trend-to-Product Engine

## Overview

This is a simple prototype designed to turn trending health and fitness topics into actionable product ideas, content angles, and subscription concepts.

## Why I Built This

Reverse Tech focuses on creating and scaling niche digital brands in the health and wellness space.

One key challenge in that model is:
**quickly identifying trends and turning them into launchable ideas.**

This tool is a small experiment to reduce the gap between:
Trend → Insight → Product Concept

## What It Does

- Collects trending health-related topics (mock data or scraped)
- Extracts recurring keywords and themes
- Generates:
  - Product ideas
  - Content hooks
  - Monetization strategies


## AI Integration

This project uses AI to transform raw trend data into structured business insights, including:

- Product ideas
- Marketing hooks
- Subscription models

The goal is to simulate how a fast-moving digital brand team can go from trend discovery to execution-ready ideas in minutes.
I have integrated two models: OpenAI and Groq. I already ran out of credits on OpenAI, so it gives me the "insufficient quota" message every time. 😄


## Example Output

- Trend: Belly Fat
- Product: 14-day belly fat challenge
- Hook: "Why your belly fat won’t go away"
- Monetization: Subscription model

## How I Think

I like building fast, testing ideas quickly, and refining based on feedback.

This is intentionally lightweight — the goal is speed and iteration, not perfection.
